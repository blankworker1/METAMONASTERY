# METAMONASTERY

AI Audio Transmission Generator 

A daily seven-minute audio transmission. Zen-inspired. Bitcoin-anchored. Fully automated. AI agent and build.

---

## What This Is

The Metamonastery is an automated philosophical broadcast -- a daily signal that appears, speaks for seven minutes, and disappears.

No archive. No replay. No host.

Each transmission is generated by an AI narrator trained to speak from inside uncomfortable questions about money, time, and human behavior. The voice is not an advocate. It is not a teacher. It is someone who has read widely, changed their mind, and remains genuinely uncertain. That uncertainty is the transmission.

The project sits at the intersection of Zen practice, monetary philosophy, and Bitcoin's structural principles. It borrows the ritual discipline of a monastery -- fixed structure, daily practice, deliberate silence -- and applies it to the most consequential questions of our economic moment.

---

## Core Principles

**Signal over noise.** Every transmission is analytical, philosophical, and grounded. No hype. No price speculation. No tribal language.

**Structure is sacred. Content varies.** Each transmission follows the same five-part architecture every day. The ritual container never changes. What it holds does.

**Frame systems, not villains.** Critique is always structural. Incentives are named. Morality is not imposed.

**Encourage doubt, not belief.** The listener is never told what to conclude. They are invited to look more carefully at what they already assume.

**Ephemerality is the point.** The transmission exists once, live, and then it is gone. Scarcity of attention is the practice.

---

## Transmission Structure

Each broadcast runs for exactly seven minutes and follows this fixed architecture:

**Opening Gong Ritual** `0:00 - 0:45`
Twenty-one slow gong strikes, approximately two seconds apart. No voice. Silence between strikes is part of the ritual. Creates presence before the transmission begins.

**Movement One -- Observation** `0:45 - 1:45`
A small, concrete observation drawn from the daily seed. Something the listener recognizes but has not examined. No thesis. No question. Just friction.

**Movement Two -- Historical and Structural Context** `1:45 - 3:45`
Real history. Named people, specific dates, actual places. Multiple schools of thought introduced without privileging any. The Austrian school, Keynesian economics, Modern Monetary Theory, and others appear as serious frameworks held by serious people. No winners declared.

**Movement Three -- Practical Reflection** `3:45 - 5:45`
One specific, verifiable action the listener is invited to try. Run a Bitcoin node. Read a primary source. Trace a belief back to its origin. The invitation is genuine, not prescriptive. Most people will not do it. That is fine.

**Movement Four -- Philosophical Anchor** `5:45 - 6:45`
Two contrasting orientations named with equal weight. One externalizes cost and trusts institutions to manage consequences. One internalizes responsibility and requires personal competence. Neither is enlightened. Neither is mistaken. The listener decides.

**Closing Signal** `6:45 - 7:00`
Five seconds of silence. One gong strike. Four words.

*Don't trust. Verify.*

## The Seed Library

`seeds.json` is the content engine of the Metamonastery. It is a curated library of starting points -- small, specific, historically grounded fragments that the AI narrator uses as the entry point for each daily transmission.

A seed is not a topic. It is a crack in the wall. The transmission enters through it and finds its own path.

Each seed entry follows this structure:

```json
{
  "id": "001",
  "category": "historical_moment",
  "seed": "Seed text goes here.",
  "themes": ["theme1", "theme2"],
  "used": false,
  "last_used": null,
  "notes": "Editorial notes for curation purposes."
}
```

**Seed categories:**

- `historical_moment` -- Specific events in monetary and financial history
- `behavioral_observation` -- Recognizable human patterns connected to structural forces
- `structural_contrast` -- Two systems or logics placed next to each other without judgment
- `primary_source_fragment` -- A line or moment from a primary text, offered without interpretation
- `contemplative_provocation` -- Open questions with no clean answer
- `bitcoin_technical_moment` -- Specific events in Bitcoin's history that illuminate broader themes

Seeds are compiled two ways: manually, from the curator's own reading and thinking, and prompt-assisted, using Claude to generate candidates that are then reviewed, edited, and curated by hand. The curator is always the editor. The AI is the research assistant.

One principle governs the library: no seed should reference a price event, a market movement, or anything that dates the transmission to a specific news cycle. A transmission from Day 1 and a transmission from Day 200 should both feel timeless when listened to a year later.

## Roadmap -- Toward Full Automation

The long-term goal is a fully automated pipeline that requires no human intervention to produce and broadcast each daily transmission. The target architecture:

**Phase 1 -- Manual Validation** *(current)*
Transmission scripts generated via webapp. Reviewed by curator. Assembled and broadcast manually. Goal: validate tone, structure, and voice consistency across 30+ transmissions.

**Phase 2 -- Partial Automation**
Scheduled script generation via Claude API. Automated TTS rendering via ElevenLabs API. Manual review gate before broadcast. Goal: remove content generation labor while preserving editorial oversight.

**Phase 3 -- Full Automation**
End-to-end pipeline triggered by daily cron job. Seed selected automatically from library. Script generated, rendered, and assembled without human intervention. Telegram bot streams assembled audio into scheduled Voice Chat. Session closes automatically. No human presence required.

**Full automation stack:**

```
Scheduler (cron / n8n / Make)
    |
    v
seeds.json  -->  Claude API (script generation)
                     |
                     v
              ElevenLabs API (TTS rendering)
                     |
                     v
              FFmpeg (audio assembly)
              [21 gong strikes + speech + silence + outro gong]
                     |
                     v
              Telegram Bot (Voice Chat broadcast)
                     |
                     v
              Session closes. No archive. Signal gone.
```

## The Narrator

The AI narrator is not a persona layered over a generic model. It is a carefully constrained voice encoded in a system prompt that has been refined across multiple iterations. The narrator:

- Speaks in first person with genuine interiority
- Has changed its mind and says so
- Asks questions it does not answer
- Names incentives without naming villains
- Treats the skeptic with complete respect
- Never uses urgency, tribal language, or price-adjacent framing
- Ends every transmission with the same four words

The system prompt is the monastery's rule. The seed is the day's practice. The transmission is what emerges when the two meet.
